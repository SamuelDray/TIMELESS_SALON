Hairstyles

Box Braids

Box braids are individual plaits that are sectioned in square-shaped “boxes” across the scalp. They can be made with synthetic extensions like Kanekalon or natural hair, and come in different sizes (thin, medium, jumbo). This style is protective, low-maintenance, and versatile — you can wear them long, in a ponytail, bun, or with accessories like beads.


  • Short to medium length: ~KSh 6,000
  • Long or more intricate: ~KSh 8,000
Conrows

Cornrows are braids that lie flat against the scalp, usually in straight or patterned rows. They’re quick to do compared to full-head extensions, great for everyday wear, and can be styled with or without added hair. This classic style is practical, protective, and works well in both casual and formal settings.


  • Basic cornrows: ~KSh 800 – 2,500 depending on complexity and length


Crochet Braids

Crochet braids are created by crocheting pre-braided or textured hair extensions into small cornrowed sections using a latch hook. They can look curly, wavy, or like individual braids depending on the hair used, and tend to be lighter weight and less time-intensive than full box braids.


  • Short to shoulder length: ~KSh 3,000
  • Mid to long length with lush curls or waves: ~KSh 5,500
  • Feed-in style with designs: ~KSh 7,000
