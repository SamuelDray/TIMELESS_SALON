Pedicure

Classic(Normal) Pedicures

This is the standard maintenance pedicure. It's perfect for someone who wants neat, clean feet without the extra frills. It usually involves a warm foot soak, nail trimming and shaping, cuticle care, a quick foot scrub (usually with a pumice stone or foot (file), a short massage, and is finished with regular nail polish.


  • KSh 1,500

Gel Pedicures

The Gel Pedicure is durable hence people often choose it. It follows the same steps as a classic pedicure but uses Gel Polish that is cured under a UV or LED lamp. This polish is smudge-proof the second you walk out and can last 3-4 weeks without chipping, which is great if you spend alot of time in open shoes.


  • KSh 2,000

Spa Pedicures

A Spa pedicure is an "upgrade" designed for maximum relaxation. On top of the basics, it includes extra treatements such as Sugar or salt scrub for deep exfoliation, a foot mask wrapped in hot towels, and an extended leg and foot massage.


  • KSh 3,500


Manicure

Classic manicure

This is the standard "clean-up" for your natural nails. It focuses on health and neatness rather than long-term wear. It includes soaking your hands in warm soapy water, trimming and shaping the nails, cuticle work (pushing back and nipping), a brief hand massage with lotion, and finishing with regular nail polish.


  • KSh 1,300
Gel Manicures

Often called "Gel Mani" or "Shellac," this is currently the most popular choice in Kenya due to its durability. The preparation is similar to a classic manicure, but instead of regular polish, a special gel-based polish is applied. Each layer is "cured" (dried) under a UV or LED lamp. It results in a high-shine finish that is completely dry the moment you leave the chair.


  • KSh 1,800


Acrylic Manicures

This is the go-to for those who want extra length or a very "hard" protective layer over their natural nails. A technician mixes a liquid (monomer) and a powder (polymer) to create a dough-like substance. This is applied over your natural nail (overlay) or over a plastic "tip" glued to the end (extensions) to add length. It hardens on its own and is then filed into your desired shape (like Coffin, Almond, or Stiletto).


  • KSh 3,500
